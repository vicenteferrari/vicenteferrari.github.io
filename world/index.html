Armarian Empire (Pronounced Ar-mah-ree-an)

A sovereign absolute theocratic monarchy. It's capital is Godstown, it is the biggest nation in Isngard and the most powerful with a raisable levy of around 14000 men The Emperor, the head of state, goverment, and the church. The Armarian faith otherwise known as "The true faith" has huge moral authority and aims to spread it's message with the sword. It is a monotheistic religion based on the god Armas. The emperor always bears the title Armar. Reffered to as His Armarian majesty, refering to both the empire and the god. It is by far the biggest religion and it has even started growing outside the empire with people willingly swearing fealty to the empire. The Empire is currently under duress from a few dukes that want to start a rebellion on accounts of lower crown authority and more decentralization. The Empire reigns over multiple cultures including the main one Armarian and the Tuliosene and Arcadian minorities, all united under "The true faith"

Godstown

Temperate climate. -5 to 5 C in winter 10 to 20 C in summer. Capital of the Armarian Empire and the biggest city in Isngard, with a population of around 100.000 people, mostly merchants and peasants live here. Known for it's fruititous and buzzling markets, it's the biggest economic hub in both the empire and the known world. In the middle of the city reside both the cathedral and the keep, both of which are ruled by the emperor, who is a living God, or so the people are told. Guards everywhere to keep the people at bay, it is very much the center of power of the empire. It sits at the end of a peninsula protecting Prince Bay.

Independant Merchant Republic of The Sisters

A sovereign merchant republic. After a long history of opression from its former overlord Arcad, the sheer financial power of the Sisterene oligarchy allowed them to barter for independence and so was established the only non-feudal state in the world. Elected every 2 years the head of state and government bears the title of Molge. With not much manpower The Sisters still present a formidable fleet, mostly used to protect their valuable trade, in lake Miriad. The adversities surrounding lake Miriad make it the most practical route to get goods from north to south and viceversa. This has made The Sisters a financial powerhouse with trade nodes all over the world thanks to The Ron, by far the widest river in the world. The sisterene are also well known for their delicious export, the Miridese cod.

Lyrel

Temperate climate. 4 to 12 C in winter 20 to 30 C in summer. Capital and only city of the I.M.R. of The Sisters. It is a sprawling (both horizontally and vertically) metropolis with a tiny permanent population, but a huge amount of day-workers from every corner of the world.
